I currently live in New York City and am looking forward to a job as a data scientist.
Healthcare Data Scientist with 3+ years of experience specializing in fraud detection, risk assessment, classification, and modelling using a national database. My PhD project uses MedicineInsight, a national database with data from over 650 general practices. It is a quality improvement program developed and managed by NPS MedicineWise with funding from the Australian Government Department of Health.
So far, I published 20+ academic papers of quantitative and qualitative research in peer-reviewed journals and international conferences. I attracted over $ 115,000 grant funding from Australia, UK, and China.
